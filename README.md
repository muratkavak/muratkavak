### About Me

I'm a final-year Computer Engineering student at Izmir Bakircay University, focused on **model-based testing** and **test automation** for safety-critical domains. Think automotive, aerospace, medical devices... the kind of software where bugs aren't just annoying, they're _dangerous_.

Currently based in **Antwerpen, Belgium**, working at the intersection of academia and industry:

- **Research Intern** at the [AnSyMo Research Group](https://ansymo.uantwerpen.be/), University of Antwerp
- **Junior Software Developer** at **SafeShift**, a university spin-off turning cutting-edge testing research into a real product

Before that, I did an **Erasmus+ semester in Poland**, which gave me a whole new perspective on life and engineering.
